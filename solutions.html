How We Can Take Action

Climate change is a global problem, but solutions begin at both the individual and societal levels. By embracing renewable energy, reducing waste, conserving forests, and shifting toward sustainable lifestyles, we can significantly slow down global warming.

Key Solutions

  • Switch to renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro)
  • Improve energy efficiency in homes and buildings
  • Adopt sustainable farming and reduce food waste
  • Plant trees and protect existing forests
  • Choose public transportation, biking, or electric vehicles
  • Support climate-friendly laws and global cooperation
