The Consequences Are Everywhere

Climate change threatens every aspect of life on Earth. Rising temperatures disrupt ecosystems, amplify natural disasters, and put millions of lives at risk every year.

Major Effects

  • Rising Sea Levels: Coastal cities are flooding more frequently.
  • Extreme Weather: Hurricanes, wildfires, and droughts are becoming more severe.
  • Melting Ice Caps: Polar regions are losing ice at record speed.
  • Threats to Wildlife: Animals lose habitats faster than they can adapt.
  • Health Risks: Heatwaves, air pollution, and water scarcity affect millions.

Understanding the effects is the first step to action.
